About SecLlama

Your Privacy-First AI Assistant

What is SecLlama?

SecLlama is a security-enhanced fork of Ollama that adds critical security layers for running large language models. It's designed for users who need maximum privacy and security when working with AI models.

Core Security Features

üîí End-to-End Encryption

All messages and chat history are encrypted using AES-256-GCM, the same encryption standard used by governments and banks. Your conversations are protected both in transit and at rest.

üîë Secure Key Storage

Encryption keys are stored in your operating system's native keychain (macOS Keychain, Linux Secret Service, Windows Credential Manager), never in plain text files.

üö´ Network Isolation

AI models run in a sandboxed environment with zero internet access. On macOS, we use sandbox-exec; on Linux, we use namespaces. Models cannot make external requests or leak data.

üíæ Local-First Architecture

Everything runs on your machine. Models, conversations, and data never leave your computer. No telemetry, no cloud sync, no external servers.

üîê Encrypted Chat History

Both CLI and GUI chat histories are encrypted before being written to disk. Even if someone gains access to your computer, they cannot read your conversations without the encryption key.

üì¶ Separate Data Storage

SecLlama uses its own data directories (~/.secllama/), allowing it to coexist with standard Ollama while maintaining complete data separation.

Why SecLlama?

  • Healthcare & Legal Professionals: Handle sensitive patient or client data with confidence
  • Journalists & Activists: Protect sources and sensitive information
  • Enterprise Users: Meet strict data security and compliance requirements
  • Privacy-Conscious Individuals: Keep your thoughts and queries completely private
  • Researchers: Work with confidential data without risk of leakage

How It Works

  1. Download & Install: Get SecLlama for macOS, Windows, or Linux
  2. Pull Models: Download AI models from the Ollama library (uses standard Ollama protocol)
  3. Chat Securely: All conversations are automatically encrypted, sandboxed, and stored locally
  4. Stay Private: Models cannot access the internet or leak your data

Encryption

Key Storage

OS-native keychains with secure access control

Data Location

~/.secllama/ for CLI | ~/Library/Application Support/SecLlama/ for GUI
